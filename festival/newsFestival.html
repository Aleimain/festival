News May 10, 2017

Hoi An…

News May 10, 2017

If you are planning a trip to Hoi An but want to decide on a good time to go, consider planning around the Hoi An Lantern Festival. This will certainly be an enchanting time to experience Hoi An and is a popular reason why visitors are drawn to Hoi An. But if you’re not in Hoi An at the exact date, remember you can still witness glowing lanterns light up the dark streets on most evenings of the year, and even opt to purchase a lantern if you wish…
